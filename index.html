Strategy Descriptions

Heating Systems

* Gas Furnace: Natural gas furnace for primary heating, electric two-speed direct expansion (DX) coil for cooling, and electric coils in VAV terminals for zone temperature control.
* Heat Pump: Electric air-to-water heat pump generating chilled and hot water for both central air handling units and zone reheat coils.

Glazing Materials

* Single Pane - Clear: single 3 mm clear pane with no coatings.
* Double Pane - Clear: double 3 mm clear panes with no coatings.
* Triple Pane - Clear: triple 3 mm clear panes with no coatings.

Shading Materials

* Exterior Shade - tvis 3% Dark : dark‑gray basket‑weave fabric shades (2 % openness factor, visible transmittance of  0.031).
* Interior Shade - tvis 3% Dark: dark‑gray basket‑weave fabric shades (2 % openness factor, visible transmittance of  0.031).

Daylight Dimming

*On: Artificial lighting dims linearly based on daylight to maintain 500 lux workplane illuminance for office tasks. When integrated with the shading control algorithm, the lighting system responds to the resulting illuminance levels from adjusted shade position.
*Off: Artificial lighting remains at full power during occupied hours regardless of daylight availability.

Control

*None: No shading control is applied.
*Manual: Always 2/3 Shaded: Windows remain consistently two-thirds shaded, simulating infrequent occupuant adjustments in typical office settings.
*Manual: Dynamic: Rule-based algorithm adjusts shades across four positions (fully open to fully closed) based on exterior vertical illuminance thresholds from 33,000 to 100,000 lux, with hysteresis to model delayed occupant responses.
*Auto: Daylight and glare control: Shades open fully when incident solar radiation is below 200 W/m² to maximize daylight; otherwise, close to minimize solar heat gain.
*Auto: Incident Radiation Control: Hierarchical algorithm adjusts shades across four positions based on solar altitude, sky conditions (cloudy or sunny), and brightness limits (3,500 lux) while maintaining thermal loads under 78 W/m² and sun penetration under 1 meter during clear sky conditions.
